Query Resolution: This will send a resolution email to the client with your response and update their status. The query history will be preserved for reference.
This response will be sent to the client via email along with Proceed/Query buttons for their next action.
Upload a document to send to the client. Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, JPG, PNG. Max size: 10MB
📋 What happens after resolving:
  • Email with your resolution will be sent to the client immediately
  • Any attached document will be included in the email as a download link

Dashboard Actions Guide

Quick reference guide for all available actions in the admin dashboard.

Approve

Confirms the appointment request, adds it to Google Calendar, and sends confirmation email to the client.

Reject

Declines the appointment request and notifies the client with the reason for rejection.

Reschedule

Allows you to propose a new date/time for the appointment. Client receives rescheduling notification.

Cancel

Cancels an approved appointment, removes it from calendar, and notifies the client.

Request Payment

Sends payment request to client with UPI details and instructions for payment proof submission.

Acknowledge Payment

Confirms receipt of payment proof from client and updates payment status.

Initiate Refund

Starts refund process by uploading refund proof and notifying client about refund initiation.

Send Consent

Sends consent form link to client for review and digital signature before session.

Send Reminder

Sends appointment reminder email to client with session details and preparation instructions.

Mark Session Done

Marks session as completed after it has been conducted. Updates status to "Session Completed".

Request Rebooking

Sends rebooking request to client for scheduling a new session after completion or cancellation.

Mark Query Resolved

Marks client query as resolved and sends resolution confirmation email to client.

Tip: Hover over action buttons to see quick tooltips. Most actions will send automated emails to clients.
